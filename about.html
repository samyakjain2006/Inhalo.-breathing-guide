About Inhalo.

I made Inhalo for people those who forget to breathe while buried in code, cramming for finals, or racing deadlines...glued to their screens for hours on end. Like me!

My goal? To make taking a healthy break feel effortless. Through simple, calming, guided breathing animations, Inhalo. helps you pause, reset, and reconnect with your breath—just for a minute or two.

I built Inhalo. during a caffeine-fueled weekend for a hackathon sprint, designed for anyone who needs to take a step back—so they can move forward with clarity.

"Inhalo. When focus meets stillness."
